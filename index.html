About Me

I am now an associate professor in the School of Computer Science & Technology, Nanjing University, China. Before joining NJU, I got my PhD from National University of Singapore in 2015, and worked as a postdoc scholar at University of California, Irvine from 2015 to 2017.

中文简介：左志强，新体制长聘副教授（特聘研究员），博士生导师。新加坡国立大学博士，美国加州大学博士后。主要研究方向为系统软件、编译器与运行时等。

Research Interests

  • System software
  • Compiler and runtime
  • Program analysis

My research interests span Programming Languages, Software Engineering, and Systems. I have been recently focusing on building and customizing systems software (e.g., compilers, runtime environments, and operating systems) for program efficiency, reliability, and safety.
