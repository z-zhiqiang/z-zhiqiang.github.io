About Me

I am now a postdoctoral scholar advised by Dr. Guoqing (Harry) Xu in Department of Computer Science, University of California, Irvine since April 2015. Before joining UCI, I pursued my PhD degree in National University of Singapore from August 2010 to February 2015 under the supervision of Dr. Siau-Cheng Khoo.

I will join the Department of Computer Science at Nanjing University as an Assistant Professor soon.

Here is my brief CV.

Research Interests

  • Automated debugging
  • Program analysis
  • Parallel and Distributed computing
  • Big Data systems

My research interests span programming languages, software engineering, and Big Data systems. I am recently focusing on building Big Data supports for scalable program analysis, synthesis and SAT solving. Moreover, I'm also interested in data mining and machine learning techniques, especially their applications on programming languages and software engineering.
